E · Radial / orbital

Current doc at the centre, predecessors on the inner ring, citations on the outer ring. Visually compelling, but cramped when the outer ring has > ~16 items.

★ H · Evolution / Lineage tree

Time flows top-to-bottom. Each row is one doc with its issue year on the left margin. The vertical spine is the chain of replaces / amends. Branches appear when multiple older docs were consolidated; the current spotlight doc is highlighted in burgundy + gold. Walks BOTH directions — back to the legal ancestors and forward to any successors. Built from the existing replaces / replacedBy data we already have, no new manual work needed.

★ I · Branching tree of evolution (SVG)

A real tree, not a list. Time flows top-to-bottom. Multiple ancestors merge into a consolidated successor (47/2010 + 17/2017 → 32/2024). Edges are colour-coded by relation type: replace (solid burgundy), amend (dashed gold), elevate (solid teal — decree raised to law). Current spotlight doc highlighted in cream + bold burgundy.
